#***** Simulation Parameters *****
#
#---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Parameter    | Value   | Description
#---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Multiprocess | False   | Whether or not to use multiprocessing
#---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Cores        | 1       | Number of cores to use for parallel processing. Positive integer
#---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Verbosity    | 0       | Verbosity for printing logging messages. 0 for low (recommended), 1 for moderate, 2 for high
#---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Experiments  | 10      | Number of Experiment MC realizations. Positive integer
#---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Observations | 10      | Number of Observation MC realizations per experiment realization. Positive integer.
#---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Detectors    | 10      | Number of Detector MC realizations per observation realization. Positive Integer.
#---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Resolution   | 0.500   | Spectral resolution for integration over bands and spectra in GHz. Positive floating point value.
#---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Foregrounds  | False   | Include Foregrounds? True or False
#---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Correlations | True    | Include white noise correlations? True or False
#---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
